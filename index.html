About Me

I am 23 years old and grew up on the Isle of Wight. I like to bake cakes for my family and watch very cheesy films with my friends.

I studied maths at the University of York and graduated in July 2020. In my third year I took part in the Erasmus programme and studied in Madrid, completely in Spanish! It was a very challenging year but I am so glad I did it.

When I was younger, I was a competitive swimmer and represented the Isle of Wight a number of times. I also worked as a lifeguard at my local pool and helped out with swimming lessons for a number of years.
